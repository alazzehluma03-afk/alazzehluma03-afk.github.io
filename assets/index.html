About Me

Data & ML Engineer

I am a Data Science and Artificial Intelligence graduate from Al al-Bayt University, currently advancing my technical expertise as an AI Engineer through the AI.SPIRE Applied AI program. My background is rooted in leveraging Python to transform complex datasets into actionable insights, with practical experience in anomaly detection and audit analytics gained during my time at ZeeDimension and Siemens Healthineers. I am driven by the challenge of building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.This passion led me to join AI.SPIRE, where I am refining my skills in end-to-end ML engineering. I am particularly interested in the mechanics of NLP systems, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and the complexities of deploying ML models to production. My goal is to bridge the gap between experimental models and scalable, production-ready AI solutions. My work so far spans git-based collaboration workflows, end-to-end data pipelines, relational databases & SQL analytics, PyTorch model training, and KPI dashboards. Each module builds toward a full-stack AI engineering skill set.

My graduation project, DeepSolar, is an intelligent solar panel fault detection system built with deep learning and computer vision — a practical example of applying AI to real-world engineering problems.

I am particularly interested in audit analytics, anomaly detection, and data pipeline engineering, and I am eager to grow in environments where data drives decisions.
